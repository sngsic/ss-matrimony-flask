SREE SUNDERASWARA TEMPLE

Sree Sundareswara Temple is one of two prominent temples consecrated by Sree Narayana Guru Devan in Kannur District, the other being Sree Jagannaatha Temple, Thalassery. The temple is located in suburban Talap in the Kannur district of Kerala, India, approximately 2 km away from Kannur Railway Station,[1] and 3 km from Kannur New Bus Stand, Thavakkara.[2] The temple has Shiva, in the form of Sundareswara, as its main deity.[3]
